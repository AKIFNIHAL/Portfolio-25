About Me

My name is Akif Nihal, and I hold a strong belief in the principles of faith and knowledge. I was born on August 20, 2000, in Dhaka, the capital of Bangladesh, which is a vibrant megacity, that has significantly shaped my perspectives and aspirations.

As an enthusiastic learner committed to academic pursuits, I have a keen interest in research and writing. Although I have yet to publish my work in formal academic venues, I actively express my insights through blogging. I value forging connections with others and possess strong emotional intelligence and communication skills, which enhance my collaborative efforts.

I travel a lot. My travels with family and friends provide me with the opportunity to explore various cultures and historical sites, thereby deepening my understanding of diverse civilizations and economic systems. My research interests are focused on technological advancements, physiological adaptations, and the socio-economic impacts of contemporary developments of innovation.

As an extroverted and inquisitive individual, I thrive on challenges and actively seek to broaden my horizons in the pursuit of knowledge. I am a firm believer in the power of education to transform lives and societies. I am passionate about contributing to the field of education and empowering others to reach their full potential. I am committed to lifelong learning and personal growth, and I strive to inspire others to do the same.

I am a dedicated and enthusiastic aerospace engineering graduate with a strong passion for the aerospace industry. I hold a Bachelor of Science in Aircraft Engineering and Maintenance Systems from the University of South Wales, where I developed a deep interest in emerging aerospace technologies, aircraft propulsion, and aviation maintenance management. My educational background, paired with my experience in research and development, has given me a solid foundation to tackle complex engineering problems.

Currently, I am work as a Quality Assurance Engineer, where I apply my meticulous attention to detail and strong problem-solving skills to ensure high-quality software systems. My technical expertise spans from aircraft maintenance to software testing, with a focus on efficiency, sustainability, and the adoption of new technologies in both domains. I am particularly interested in the potential of electric aircraft and 3D printing technologies to revolutionize the aerospace industry.

In addition to my technical experience, I have honed my leadership and teamwork skills through active participation in the Leo Club of Dhaka Frontier and various roles in the Lions Club International. I value professional standards and commitment to society, constantly striving to bring a blend of innovation, ethics, and collaboration to every project I am part of.
